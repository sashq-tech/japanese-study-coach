Privacy

Private by design.

Japan Ready Coach currently runs in the browser with no account system, backend database, analytics, or external API calls.

What Is Stored

The app stores progress in browser local storage: scores, streaks, kana mastery, weak-item review, selected study focus, mini-session summaries, timer history, and local human review notes.

Where It Stays

That data stays in the browser and device where it was created. It is not sent to a server by this static study app. Clearing browser storage can delete progress.

Your Controls

The app includes export, import, and reset controls for local progress. Export a backup before switching browsers, clearing cache, or moving to another device.

Advertising Cookies

If ads are shown on Japan Ready Coach, third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this site or other sites. Google's advertising cookies allow Google and its partners to personalize ads based on visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

Ad Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings. You can also visit aboutads.info for opt-out choices from some third-party advertising vendors.
