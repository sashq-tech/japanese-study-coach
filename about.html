About

Japan Ready Coach

A static, local-first prototype I am building to rebuild my Japanese from the beginning and prepare for real life in Japan.

Why It Exists

I have spent money on Japanese books and apps, then drifted away because the practice did not feel personal enough to keep using. Japan Ready Coach is my attempt to build the study path I am more likely to stick with.

Human Context

I lived near Yokota AB from 1995 to 2001, then later near Kadena AB in Okinawa from 2007 to 2010. My son was born in Okinawa in 2008, and my wife Emi is Japanese and visits Japan yearly.

Current Perspective

Some of my own Japan experience is older, so Emi's current perspective matters. I want this site to combine beginner study with practical notes about payment, transportation, luggage, etiquette, and small costs.

Purpose

This project is designed to help a beginner start gently with hiragana, katakana, and JLPT N5 basics before moving into harder material. The current app favors short practice loops, review, and daily continuity over long lessons.

Learning Scope

The current scope is kana-first N5 starter practice: recognition, vocabulary, particles, grammar patterns, sentence checks, and practical phrases. It is not a complete JLPT preparation product.

Accuracy Boundary

Japanese explanations and examples should be treated as learning aids that need review. The project does not guarantee language accuracy, naturalness, or official JLPT alignment.
